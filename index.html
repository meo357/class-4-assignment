NYC Community Food Connection Centers

New York City’s Department of Social Services distributes funding to over 700 community kitchens and food pantries citywide under their Community Food Connection (CFC) program. Ranging from churches to formal food distribution sites, these locations are the primary distribution centers for emergency food assistance.
